Help & Support

Frequently asked questions and guides

Getting Started

1. Create a Course: Start by creating a treatment course in the Courses section.

2. Log Injections: Use the "Log Injection" button on the dashboard to record your injections.

3. Track Symptoms: Record any side effects or symptoms to keep a comprehensive history.

Managing Inventory

Go to the Inventory page to add your medications and supplies.

You can set low stock alerts and track expiration dates to ensure you never run out.

Reports

Generate PDF reports to share with your healthcare provider.

Export your data as CSV for your own records or analysis.

Account Sharing

Invite a partner to manage your tracking together.

Go to Settings > Account & Sharing to generate an invite link.
